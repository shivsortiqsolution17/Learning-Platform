Our Story

We Built the School We Wished Existed

LearnFlow started in 2021 when three educators got tired of watching talented people stall in outdated bootcamps and passive video libraries. We believed learning should be active, mentored, and outcome-driven.

So we built a platform that combines expert instruction, community accountability, and AI-guided paths. Today we're a team of 38, helping over 42,000 learners around the world build skills employers actually value.
